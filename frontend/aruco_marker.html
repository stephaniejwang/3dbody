ArUco Marker for 3D Body Measurement

How to use:
  1. Click "Print" below (or use Ctrl+P / Cmd+P).
  2. Print at 100% scale (no fit-to-page). The marker must be exactly 10cm x 10cm.
  3. Cut out the marker along the border.
  4. When recording your video, hold this marker visibly in frame.
  5. The marker should be flat and well-lit for best detection.
